How to use this site

  1. Pick a card and read the short story.
  2. Check the Bible references in your Bible.
  3. Compare choices using the status badge and “Remember this” lesson.
  4. Use the map and timeline to connect people, places, and events.

Accuracy note

This is a Bible-first learning tool. Ancient dates are shown as approximate when historians differ, while names, sequence, and story summaries point back to the listed Scripture references.

Status badges

Good means the biblical narrative broadly commends the king. Bad marks kings described as doing evil. Mixed identifies complicated stories with both faithfulness and failure. Foreign marks non-Israelite rulers.
